Enter inputs and click Calculate ROI.
Benefit components: (1) Retention = churned × avg value; (2) Acquisition = lost prospects × avg value; (3) OpEx savings = (cases prevented × 50%) × [(ops hrs × ops cost) + (CS hrs × CS cost)].
Costs: total subscription cost = per-user subscription × active customers.
ROI (IDR) = Benefits − Costs.
ROI (%) = ROI / (Per-user subscription × Active customers). (May exceed 100% when benefits greatly exceed costs.)
